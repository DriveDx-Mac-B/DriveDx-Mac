## Understanding the Four Health Statuses

One idea worth grasping early: <a href="#">DriveDx for mac</a> does not just say a drive is fine or
broken. It reports four states — Ok, Warning, Failing (pre-failure) and Failed — so you get a graduated
warning instead of a sudden death. A Warning tells you an attribute has drifted; a Failing status is
DriveDx predicting that this drive is on its way out. That extra resolution is the whole point: it buys
you time to back up and swap the drive before you lose anything.
